# Commercial licensing

TBD-16 firmware is open source under the **GNU GPL v3.0** (see `LICENSE`). That
means: if you build a product on it, your product's complete corresponding
source must be made available under the GPL. The **WebUI** is separately
**proprietary** (© dadamachines / Johannes Elias Lohbihler) and is not open
source at all.

If that doesn't fit your product, dadamachines offers a **commercial licence**:

- **Closed-source firmware** — modify and ship TBD-derived firmware without the
  GPL's source-disclosure obligations.
- **Ship without attribution / "powered by" requirements** (within reason).
- **Use of the WebUI** in your product.
- **Custom OEM terms**, support, and — where applicable — coverage of the
  upstream CTAG engine and of GPL-licensed dependencies (see `THIRD-PARTY.md`).
- We also offer **TBD-Core** (a ready core DSP board) and **Custom Integration**
  (the ESP32-P4 + RP2350 + codec on your PCB), and **licensed special editions**
  of the TBD-16.
