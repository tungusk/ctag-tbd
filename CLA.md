## Why this exists

The TBD platform is offered under a **dual licence**: the GNU GPL v3.0 for
everyone, **and** a commercial licence for parties who want to build
closed-source products on it (see `LICENSE`). For that to work, the project
needs the right to distribute every contribution under *both* licences. A plain
"my PR is GPL too" grant isn't enough — hence this CLA. You keep the copyright
in your work; you grant dadamachines a broad licence to use it.

This is the same model used by JUCE, Qt, Grafana, and many other dual-licensed
projects.
